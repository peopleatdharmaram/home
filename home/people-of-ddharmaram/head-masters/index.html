About Sri Pentaparthy Srikanth Reddy HM ZPHS D.Dharmaram (2006-2009)
 
Sri Pentaparthy Srikanth Reddy garu served as the  Head Master for ZPHS D.Dharmaram during 2006 Till 2009. His father Sri. P. Anjireddy garu also served as the teacher for decades at  ZPHS D.Dharmaram.
Sri P. Srikanth Reddy sir's remarkable contributions towards bringing out the old records of the  ZPHS D.Dharmaram was appreciated by many people of D.Dharmaram.
