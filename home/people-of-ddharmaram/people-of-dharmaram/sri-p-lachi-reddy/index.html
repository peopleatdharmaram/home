About P. Lachi Reddy
Sri Pentaparthi Lachi Reddy  is the sarpanth of D.Dharmaram village. Both of his sons, Sri P. Somasekhara Reddy and Sri P. Rajashekara Reddy are IAS Officers, completed their schooling at ZPHS D.Dharmaram
