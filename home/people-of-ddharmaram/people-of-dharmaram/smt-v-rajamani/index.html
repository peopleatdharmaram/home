Smt V Rajamani w/o Sri Vuduta Siddaiah  is the first women sarpanch of D.Dharmaram village.
She was elected by the  people of D.Dharmaram. Smt V Rajamani is from TDP.

Sarpanches of D.Dharmaram   
  1. Sri Venkat Ram Reddy
  2. Sri Vuduta Nagaiah
  3. Sri P. Lachi Reddy
  4. Sri Vuduta Siddaiah 
  5. Sri Masaipet Ramaiah 
  6. Smt. V. Rajamani(2001-05)
  7. Sri. Ranga Goud(2005-2011)
