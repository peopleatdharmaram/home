Sri Satyanarayana Sir(1937-2011) spent most of his lifetime teaching the students of  ZPHS D.Dharmaram. Satyanarayana sir born at Chittapur near Siddipet in Medak district. 

Sir completed his schooling from Dubbak and Ramayampet High schools, completed the Hindi Vidwan and became Hindi Pandit.

Out of 45 years of service in many schools including Ramayampet girls and boys high school and Brahmanapally, sir spent about 25 years at ZPHS D.Dharmaram, it is no surprise that Sir has worked with almost all of the teaching staff at ZPHS D.Dharmaram. We always find him in the photographs of most of the passed out groups of ZPHS D.Dharmaram.

Earlier after working for about 10 years at ZPHS D.Dharmaram, sir came back to ZPHS D.Dharmaram in the year 1989-90 and worked at ZPHS D.Dharmaram till his retirement in 1999-2000.

Sir passed away on August 30th 2011 due to heart related illness. The  people of D.Dharmaram and the students of ZPHS D.Dharmaram will remember sir in their hearts forever.
