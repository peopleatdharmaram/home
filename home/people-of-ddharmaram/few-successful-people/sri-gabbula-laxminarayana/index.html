Sri. Gabbula Laxminarayana

Sri Dr.Laxminarayana Gabbula garu is son of late  Sri Ramalingam and younger brother of Late Sri G. Jaganatham sir and Gabbula krishnamurthy sir.

Sri Dr.Laxminarayana Gabbula garu has completed B.E(ECE) from Osmania university , M.Tech from IISc Bangalore and Ph.D from JNTU Hyderabad. Sri Dr.Laxminarayana Gabbula garu has worked in CCMB, OU and presently working as principal and prof. of an engineering college in Hyderabad.
Sri Dr.Laxminarayana Gabbula garu is a student of the ZPHS D.Dharmaram school , passed 7th  class in 1967. Sri Dr.Laxminarayana Gabbula's few classmates  in DDM are Datrika Arjunrao, Seelam Hanumanthreddy, molugh siddaiah etc...
