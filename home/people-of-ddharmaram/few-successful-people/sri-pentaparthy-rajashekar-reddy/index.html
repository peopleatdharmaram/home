Sri P. Rajasekhara Reddy is currently Special secretary to CM Telangana from D.Dharmaram village and also a student of ZPHS D.Dharmaram. His brother, Sri P. Somasekhara Reddy is an IAS officer. Sri P. Somasekhara Reddy and Sri P. Rajashekara Reddy are sons of Sri P. Lachi Reddy the Ex. Sarpanch of D.Dharmaram village. Many students and people of D.Dharmaram taken these two brothers as an inspiration and trying to reach the best at their lives. 

Sri P. Rajashekara Reddy who was previously with the Union labour ministry as Regional Provident Fund Commissioner, Hyderabad, is now with Mr K Chandra Shekar Rao, the Chief Minister of Telangana on a role called Special secretary to CM Telangana. Sri P. Rajashekara Reddy worked with Mr K Chandra Shekar Rao when he was the Union Labor Minister in the UPA-1 Government.
