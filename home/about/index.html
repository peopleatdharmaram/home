About D.Dharmaram
D.Dharmaram is one of the peaceful villages of India,  reflecting the true spirit and culture of indian village and situated at just about 80 Kms from the state capital, Hyderabad.
 also called as Dongala Dharmaram (డి. ధర్మారం , దొంగల ధర్మారం) is situated (view in map) about 10 KMs from the mandal headquarterRamayampet, Medak district in Telangana state of India. D.Dharmaram is about80KMs from the state capital, Hyderabad. The village PIN Code is 502102.The Name, Dongala Dharmaram came to this village because of the big forest just adjacent to the Dharmaram village (Know More).
About This Temple
Swayambhu, Bhiknoor Sri Siddarameshwara Temple is around 20 Kms from D.Dharmaram village, around 10Kms from Ramayampet, 15 Kms from Kamareddy, around 90 Kms from Hyderbad on the National Highway, NH-44 (formally NH-7) from Kashi to Kanyakumari.

It's very old temple, famous for it's reverse position of the linga. There is a big bell in the temple. This temple is very known for it's power, many devotees come to this temple every year. Know more about this temple.
