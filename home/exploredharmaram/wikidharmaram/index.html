[edit]About D.Dharmaram, Location

pedda tuumu

Dharmaram village is situated in Medak district with Ramayampet as its mandal head quarter. It is located about 10 Kms away from Ramayampet on the state highway connecting Ramayampet and Siddipet and well connected by buses and auto-rickshaws. if you want more details please visit our website sites.google.com/site/dharmaramschools

[edit]Population

he main road in Dharmaram

Its population is around 5,000 dominated by Mudirajas, which is a backward caste, followed by the schedule castes.

[edit]People

Gram Panchayat office and water tank

People in the village are mostly dependent on agriculture with little or no non-farm activity. Infrastructurally it is well developed with a high school, primary health center, veterinary hospital, milk collection center and a sugar factory. There used to be a branch of Manjeera Grameena Bank but an extremist group blew it off in 1996. District Rural Development Agency has been instrumental in promoting SHGs in the village. There are presently two beedi making units, which employs large number of women.

[edit]ZPHS High School

ZPHS(Zilla Parishat High School), Dharmaram is one of the oldest schools in Medak district. Recently new model building was constructed for the high school.The total school strength is 412. This school is currently having NIIT computer lab with 12 computers. In this lab, students are regularly practicing computer and internet concepts.The school is having internet facility also.
