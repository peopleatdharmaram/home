Hanuman Temple, D.Dharmaram

Sri Hanuman Temple at D.Dharmaram
Sri Hanuman Temple  is the most famous temples of D.Dharmaram village, this temple is situated in the heart of the village and most of the people of this village regularly perform pujas and prayers to the Hanuman of this temple.  
During the day Vinayaka Chavithi Celebrations on first day, all the Ganesh idols will be kept at this temple and once the puja is performed the idols of Ganesha will be taken to their respective ganesh mandals.
You may be also see the other three Hanuman Temples in D.Dharmaram. 
1. The Hanuman Temple near Shivini Gudi
2. The Hanuman Temple near D.Dharmaram Alugu
3. The Hanuman Temple inside D.Dharmaram village
