Shivuni Gudi

Shiva Temple, D.Dharmaram(శివుని గుడి)
History
Shivuni Gudi was constructed very long back. Sri Jangam Prabhu Rajam and his wife used to clean the perform daily puja to this Shivuni gudi during  mid 1950's. He was the person who made this temple to the normal usage the people of Dharmaram.

The Shiva Temple (శివుని గుడి), is in the heart of the D.Dharmaram village. It was constructed very long back. During the Dasara, all the villagers of D.Dharmaram will come to this temple and performs prayers. On the day of Ugadi, Panchanga Sravanam(పంచాంగ శ్రవణం) will take place in the premises of this temple.
