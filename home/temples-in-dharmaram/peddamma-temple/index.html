Peddamma Temple

Peddamma Temple(పెద్దమ్మ దేవాలయము)    
    Peddamma Temple(పెద్దమ్మ దేవాలయము) of D.Dhrmaram is constructed by the people of D.Dharmam and specifically the mudiraajas of this village. This temple is constructed couple of years back. This temple is started by the astavadhani, Sri Astaka Narasimha Rama Sharma, who built the Sri Anantha Sagar Saraswathi Keshetramu, near to Siddipet.
    Every year, jaatara is celebrated by the people of this village, D.Dharmaram. During the day of the Jaatara, from each home of the Muduraj community will perform puja and maintain fasting and takes the Bonalu upto this temple during the evening time as a very big procession.  This temple is situated at the entrance of the village just beside to the Ramayampet to Gajwel main road.
