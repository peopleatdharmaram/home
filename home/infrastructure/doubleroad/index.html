The much awaited Ramayampet to Gajwel double road works via D.Dharmaram village has been started. People of this village are excited with this development initiative. This project has been approved with the efforts from D.Dharmaram MLA, state deputy speaker Smt Padma Devender Reddy and the irrigation minister Sri Harish Rao, here is the  news article in Namaste Telangana on Octover 29th 2015.
