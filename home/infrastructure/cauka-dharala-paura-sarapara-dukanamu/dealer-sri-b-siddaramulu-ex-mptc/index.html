చౌక ధరల  పౌర సరపర దుకాణము(Dealer Sri B.Siddaramulu Ex MPTC)
There are two ration shops are available  Locally these are known as " Ration Shops" and commonly sell wheat, rice, kerosene and sugar at a price lower than the market price. However, other essential commodities may also be sold. These are also called Fair Price Shops. For buying items from this shop one must have Ration Card.
