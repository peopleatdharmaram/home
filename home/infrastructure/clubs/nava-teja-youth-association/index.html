Navateja Youth Association formed by the young, spirited youth of D.Dharmaram village. They have organized, celebrated various events in the D.Dharmaram. They have taken responsibilities of Raghupathi Gutta Jaatara - 2014 (రఘుపతి గుట్ట జాతర) celebrations, installed a permanent Saraswathi Maata vigraham at ZPHS, started and successfully running the D.Dharmaram Library, installed costly street lights with their own expenses and actively participates and celebrated various events of D.Dharmaram including Holi, Ganesh Navaratri Ustav etc.

Members of the Navateja Youth Association are Pitla Prabhakar, Srinivas Nngunuri, Suresh Tallapally, Mohammed Rizwan  Mohammed Rizwan, Servent Ramesh, Sai Dileep Krishna Gadipally, Mallangari Santhosh Reddy, Praveen Vadla and Badri Shekar. 

In the year 2014, Navateja Youth Association taken up the organization responsibilities of Raghupathi Gutta Jaatara - 2014 (రఘుపతి గుట్ట జాతర) celebrations and they have executed it successfully. 



 




Navateja Youth Association taken up a very big initiation by establishing a permanent idol of Sri Sarawathi Maata on the holy premises of ZPHS D.Dharmaram High school building.
