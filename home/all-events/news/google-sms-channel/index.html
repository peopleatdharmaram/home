Google SMS Channel

 
Naveen Kumar Chouki, a resident of D.Dharmaram has created a Google News Channel by the name, DharmaramInfo. You can subscribe to this news channel to get the news update of D.Dharmaram village on your mobile phone.
To subscribe to this news channel from internet, click here.
To subscrive to this news channel from your mobile, type "ON DharmaramINFO" and send it to 9870807070. It is free of cost and you will receive news in the form of SMS.
