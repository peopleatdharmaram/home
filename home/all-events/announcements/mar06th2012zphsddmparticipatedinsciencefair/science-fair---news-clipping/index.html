Mar 06th 2012: ZPHS DDM Participated in Science Fair
The students of ZPHS D.Dharmaram participated in the Science Fair conducted at mandal headquarter, Ramayampet  on 5th March 2012. The students of ZPHS D.Dharmaram along with the  HM, Sri D. Venkateshwarao Rao participated in this event. Here is the related News clipping of this article posted on 06th March 2012.
