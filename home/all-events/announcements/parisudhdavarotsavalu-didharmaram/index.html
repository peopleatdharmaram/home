పారిశుద్ద వారొత్సవాలు - డి. ధర్మారం

Reported by Naveen Kumar Chouki, D.Dharmaram
 
On 17th June 2014,  people of D.Dharmaram celebrated Parishudda Vaarostavalu, a program led by the Sarpanch of the village, Sri B. Siddaramulu and the other officals of the mandal headquarters.






Welcome to the News section of D.Dharmaram village website.
