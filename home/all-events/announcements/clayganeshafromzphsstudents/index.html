Clay Ganesha by the students of ZPHS Dharmaram! 
On 19th Sept 2012, 2012-13 SSC  students of ZPHS D.Dharmaram made an ganesh Idol here in D.Dharmaram.
This news article also got published in Namaste Telangaana Daily today on 20th Sept 2012. Click here for more details and the news article.

About D.Dharmaram 
Dharmaram, also called as Dongala Dharmaram (డి. ధర్మారం , దొంగల ధర్మారం) is situated (view in map) about 10 KMs from the mandal headquarterRamayampet, Medak district in Telangana state of India. D.Dharmaram is about80KMs from the state capital, Hyderabad. The village PIN Code is 502102.The Name, Dongala Dharmaram came to this village because of the big forest just adjacent to the Dharmaram village (Know More).
How to Reach Dharmaram?
NH-7 is now called as NH-44 is just 10 KMs away from Dharmaram village, while coming from Hyderabad, take a right turn after Chegunta town which is on highway. You can also reach D.Dharmaram village from Ramayampet which is about 10 KMs. D.Dharmaram is about 80Kms away from Hyderabad, the Telangana state capital. Know more here ..
