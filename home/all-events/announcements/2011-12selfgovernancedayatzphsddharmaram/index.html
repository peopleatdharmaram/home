2011-12 Self Governance Day at ZPHS D.Dharmaram

The  students of ZPHS D.Dharmaram celebrated the Self Governance Day at  ZPHS D.Dharmaram on 16th Feb 2012. On this occation,  T.Beergonda, CH. Ishwarya  and J.Sowmya, the student of ZPHS D.Dharmaram 2011-12 batch were awareded with the memontos for their impressive academic performance. The awards were given by the HM, Sri D. Venkateshwarao Rao.
