Events

Since years, there are many things happened in Dharmaram village, here we are trying to list few events happened in this village. 
Village Events
In D.Dharmaram  village there are couple of events, festivals and Jaataras celebrated with lot of passion and happiness. Here are the key events and jaararas celebrated every year in Dharmaram
    Yearly Event
         2014 Yearly Events in D.Dharmaram
Holi Festival - 2014 (హొళి పండగ) is one of the biggest events in the D.Dharmaram and celebrated with greater spirit by the people of D.Dharmaram.

Raghupathi Gutta Jaatara - 2014 (రఘుపతి గుట్ట జాతర) is one of the biggest celebrations in the D.Dharmaram and celebrated with greater spirit by the people of D.Dharmaram.  This time the well spirited youth club, Navateja Youth Association taken up the organization responsibilities of this year celebrations and they have executed it successfully this year.

         2013 Yearly Events in D.Dharmaram
2013 వినాయక నవరాత్రులు at D.Dharmaram: In D.Dharmaram, people celebrate this festival with lot of interest and respect. This is one of the rarest festivals in D.Dharmaram celebrated with lot of spirit, enthusiasm and interest.

2013 Devi Navaratri Ustav at D.Dharmaram:  Like every year in D.Dharmaram, people of this village celebrated the nine day long devi navaratrulu near the Kashir with lots of devotion and respect this year in 2013. This time the devi idol looking more beautiful and amazing, people came for the dussera festival visited the maata and taken the blessing. As per the tradition, on the next of the  dussera festival, the the goddess imersion will takes place. Since years, the people of D.Dharmaram always stands in front when it comes to celebrating the festivals and spiritual events.


        Ellamma Jaatara    ( జాతర)
        Peddama Jaatara(పెదమ్మ జాతర)
On 2011 May 29th, D.Dharmaram people especially the mudiraj community has celebrated, "Peddamma Jaatara" grandly in the village. This five days event was celebrated with Boonalu and Bandlu happily by the people of this village.
