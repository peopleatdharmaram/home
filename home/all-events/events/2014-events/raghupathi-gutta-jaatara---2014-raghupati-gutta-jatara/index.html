Raghupathi Gutta Jaatara - 2014 (రఘుపతి గుట్ట జాతర) is one of the biggest celebrations in the D.Dharmaram and celebrated with greater spirit by the people of D.Dharmaram.  This time the well spirited youth club, Navateja Youth Association taken up the organization responsibilities of this year celebrations and they have executed it successfully this year.
