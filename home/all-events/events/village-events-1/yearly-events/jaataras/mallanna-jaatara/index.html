Mallanna Jaatara(మల్లన్న జాతర)

Every year, there will be Mallanna Jaataram in the village, Dharmaram. This jaatara is celebrated by all the villagers of the D.Dharmaram and organized by theGolla community of this village once in every year. The events related to this festival takes place around 1-2 days before and after the main jaatara day.
