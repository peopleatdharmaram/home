How to reach D.Dharmaram village? 
D.Dharmaram village is in the Ramayampet mandal, Medak district, Telangana state, India. To reach dharmaram village the nearest big city is Hyderabad in India. From Hyderabad, this village will be around 80 KMs. 

By Road
To reach D.Dharmaram by road from Hyderabad, there is a national highway which pass through very closely from Dharmaram.      
    
From Hyderabad take NH-7 Highway till the endi
ng junction of Chegunta town. At the end of the chegunta junction, you find a small crossing to the right (when you are coming from Hyderabad) which is shown the right side photograph.  
From there you need to travel around 10KMs to reach dharmaram village. 

These are the few photographs taken near that NH-7 and Dharmaram road starting crossing. It takes around 1 hour 30 minutes approximately to reach the village from hyderabad by car.  To reach the Dharmaram village from Hyderabad through the public transport, you need to take APSRTC bus from the Jubli bus station(JBS) at platform number 4 with any bus with the destination as Ramayampet, Kamareddy, Nizamabad, Armoor, Basar, Nanded, Nirmal, Baynsa. You need to getdown from the bus at Ramayampet. From ramayampet autorikshaws are available to Dharmara 
village with nominal prices.


By Train
• Nearest rail station is Akanpet (Station Code AKE), the distance from Akanpet to Dharmaram via Ramayampet is around 13KMs.
• Nearest major railway station is Kamareddy (Station Code KMC).
