Ibrahimpur Sanstanam

Dharmaram was under the rule of the Ibrahimpur Sanstanam, currently the Ibrahimpur village around 7 KMs away, was under the rule of Satavahana, VishnuKumdin, Western Chalukya, Rastakuta, Yadhava, Kakathiya, Qutub Shahi and Nizam kings respectively. Accoording to the history of this region it was also found that there were contacts with Romans too.
