Where the History resides..
This is the Ghadi.The village Dharmaram was under the rule of Ibrahimpur Sanstanam, currently called as Ibrahimpur village, which is around 7 KMs away from D.Dharmaram. This is The Ghadi, where the raaja from Ibrahimpur Sanstanam used to come and stay.Ibrahimpur Sanstanam was under the rule of Satavahana, VishnuKumdin, Western Chalukya, Rastakuta, Yadhava, Kakathiya, Qutub Shahi and Nizam kings respectively. Accoording to the history of this region it was also found that there were contacts with Romans.
