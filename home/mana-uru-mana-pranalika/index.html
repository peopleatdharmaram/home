Let us decide our future of our village
మన ఊరు మన ప్రణాళిక program has started by the chief minister of Telangana, Sri K Chandra Shekar Rao today on 13th July 2014. Let us actively participant in this initiation and let us decide what we need for our village.

Here are the few thoughts started by the people of the village of D.Dharmaram:

#1: A pure drinking water project, where everyone can buy mineral water for just 2-5 Rs a bottle, if govt starts a mineral water plant?
#2: More trees, good sevarage facilities, toilets for all?
#3: Solar subsidy to get power inverters to get 24 hrs power supply to homes?
#4: A full fledged government library with a librarian, with all magazines, books, news papers including english village?
#5: Solar Street lights to whole village with powerful lights at all major junctions in the village?
#6: A computer center with internet facility with free access to all the students and youth of the village to prepare for competitive exams and get access to world?
#7. Let us take Ravinder input. A clean road til Raghupathi Gutta
#8: A mini sports complex/stadium with all major sports kits for the youth to participant in all games & events
#9: A Government fuld for a Youth center building having library, sports center, study hall all in once place with atleast 2-5 computers with internet connectivity?
#10: Two lines Road connecting Ramayampet to Gajwel to bring the major connectivity & transportation via our village?
#11: A direct/better road to National Highway instead of 80 turns road?
#12: A meditation hall with Study center for all the students to come and make a very good study circle?
#13: A government funded full fledged Gym with all equipments?
#14: A permanent water harvesting plan to improve the ground water to solve major water problems for the farmars of the village?
kindly continue thinking.. nothing harm in expressing our needs
who knows our village may get enough funds to develop our own village?

About D.Dharmaram
Dharmaram, also called as Dongala Dharmaram (డి. ధర్మారం , దొంగల ధర్మారం) is situated (view in map) about 10 KMs from the mandal headquarter Ramayampet, Medak district in Telangana state of India. D.Dharmaram is about 80KMs from the state capital, Hyderabad. The village PIN Code is 502102.The Name, Dongala Dharmaram came to this village because of the big forest just adjacent to the Dharmaram village (Know More).
