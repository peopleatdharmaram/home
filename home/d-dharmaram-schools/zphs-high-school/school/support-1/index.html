Where is ZPHS Dharmaram?

This school is located in D.Dharmaram village of Ramyamapet mandal of Medak district in Telangana of India. D.Dharmaram ZPHS school is one of the oldest schools in Medak district. ZPHS Dharmaram school was established before 1953. Till 1953, in Dharmaram there was an Upper primary school running. 

History of ZPHS Dharmaram
Dharmaram School is one of the oldest school in Medak Dist. The first UPS School Head Master is Sri Jagannatham sir garu. When this school was started, it was initially managed in some small huts. After that the school was run in a small private house. After couple of years, the own school building was constructed with 3 rooms. After some years few more RCC class rooms were constructed. Recently, in 2008 a new double store building was constructed and it is yet to be inaugurated. 



About D.Dharmaram
Dharmaram, also called as Dongala Dharmaram (డి. ధర్మారం , దొంగల ధర్మారం) is situated (view in map) about 10 KMs from the mandal headquarterRamayampet, Medak district in Telangana state of India. D.Dharmaram is about80KMs from the state capital, Hyderabad. The village PIN Code is 502102.The Name, Dongala Dharmaram came to this village because of the big forest just adjacent to the Dharmaram village (Know More).
