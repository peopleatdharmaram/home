Talent Hunt For 10th class students

Career Guidance follows

On 19th June 2010, The Talent Hunt was conducted successfully by the old students of this school. Students shown very good performance in the examination. The highest marks secured were 19 out of 30 marks. The exam was conducted in 3 areas, mathematics, physical sciences and logical reasoning.
