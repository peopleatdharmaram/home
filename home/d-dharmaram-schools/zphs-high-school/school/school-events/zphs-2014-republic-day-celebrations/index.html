2014 Jan 26 -  ZPHS D.Dharmaram  Republic Day Celebrations completed successfully with great participantion from disciplined students of  ZPHS D.Dharmaram  under the great leadership of the PET, Laxman Sir, Head master of  ZPHS D.Dharmaram.

1996-97 SSC Batch old Students of  ZPHS D.Dharmaram  sponsored about 300 prizes for the various events conducted on the eve of Republic Day.
