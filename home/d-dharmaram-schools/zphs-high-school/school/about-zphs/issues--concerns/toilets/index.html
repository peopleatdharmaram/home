Toilets Problem

The major problem the students, staff of ZPHS D.Dharmaram is lack of toilets. The main sufferers of this problem is, the girl students and the lady staff members. In this school, there is no facility of latrines and urinals. ZPHS Dharmaram is one of the oldest schools in Medak districts and lots of students come to study at this school from nearby surrounding villages like Shivaipally, Sutharpally, Venkatapoor, Pulimamidi and Kishtapoor. So one can understand easily how much problematic if there is no problem toilet facility.
