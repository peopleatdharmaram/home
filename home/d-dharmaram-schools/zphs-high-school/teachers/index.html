The Teachers of ZPHS D.Dharmaram
There are so many teachers worked at ZPHS D.Dharmaram and left their everlasting impressions in the hearts of many successful students. Come, let's start exploring about beloved teachers of ZPHS D.Dharmaram.

గురుబ్రహ్మ, గురుర్విష్ణుః, గురుర్దేవో మహేశ్వరఃగురుస్సాక్షాత్ పరబ్రహ్మ తస్మై శ్రీ గురవే నమః

In the D.Dharmaram, hundreds of teachers taught many students since early 1910's when D.Dharmaram school has started. There are many teachers who inspired the students and they are still remembered by the people of D.Dharmaram. Here is a small attempt to bring details of few teachers from the many. If you want to share your thoughts and experiences about a teacher who inspired the most at D.Dharmaram, please send your notes to email@ddharmaram.com
