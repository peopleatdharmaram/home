About ZPHS Dharmaram
ZPHS D.Dharmaram, one of the oldest school in Medak Dist is located inD.Dharmaram village of Ramyamapet mandal of Medak district in Telangana of India. This school was upgraded to High school in the year 1953. Recently, in 2008 this school got moved into a double store building. This school is currently having NIIT computer lab with 12 computers.
About D.Dharmaram
Dharmaram, also called as Dongala Dharmaram (డి. ధర్మారం , దొంగల ధర్మారం) is situated (view in map) about 10 KMs from the mandal headquarterRamayampet, Medak district in Telangana state of India. D.Dharmaram is about 80KMs from the state capital, Hyderabad. The village PIN Code is 502102.
