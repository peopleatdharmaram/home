1996-97 SSC Students Meet - Gurusanmanam

పూర్వ విద్యార్థులు సమ్మేళనం New

On 16th May 2010, the 1996-97 SSC students of this ZPHS D.Dharmaram school are celebrated students meet and facilitated their teachers with Gurusanmanam program.
The program is started in the morning around 8:30 AM. After having the breakfast students gathered at the school grounds. Students introduced themselves and they participated enthusiastically in the games cunducted by themselves and by the time all teachers reached the shool it is almost sunny afternoon around 1 PM. After the lunch, students invited their teachers on to the stage and shown their respect towards their teachers by facilitating them with Gurusanmanam. After than all teachers recollected their sweet memories they had when they were teachers at ZPHS D.Dharmaram. Each student was given a chance to speak a few words and almost all students really became emotional when everyone is recalled their past memories with the school, staff and their friends. It was a wonderful evening and we have taken a nice photograph of all students and staff at the same place where we had taken our last group photo about 14 years back.
