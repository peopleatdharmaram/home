Ibrahimpur Village

D.Dharmaram to Ibrahimpur village

Google Map
Ibrahimpur village is around 7 KMs away from D.Dharmaram village and it comes under Chegunta mandal of Medak district.
During the olden days, D.Dharmaram was under the rule of the Ibrahimpur Sanstanam, was under the rule of Satavahana, VishnuKumdin, Western Chalukya, Rastakuta, Yadhava, Kakathiya, Qutub Shahi and Nizam kings respectively. Accoording to the history of this region it was also found that there were contacts with Romans too.
