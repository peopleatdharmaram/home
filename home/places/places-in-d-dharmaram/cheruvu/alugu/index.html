Alugu

Alugu at D.Dharmaram Cheruvu
Places near Alugu
1. Temple : We have a temple near the alugu. People of this village performs pujas and prayers to this temple mostly during their family events like weddings and gruhapravesams.

2. We have a water purification unit just near the Alugu.
This purification unit supplies the pure drinking water to the people of Dharmaram and it's surrounding villages like Pulimamidi, Kistapur, Bonala, Kondapur, Shivaypalli and Sutarpally.
Click here to see details about this unit under Dharmaram Infrastructure section.
3. We have a Hanuman Temple near to the Alugu. This temple is also an old temple in Dharmaram. The people of Dharmaram visits this temple during the Jambi on the day of Dussera.
