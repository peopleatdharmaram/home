Pedda Tuumu


Pedda Tuum is in the Dharmaram chervu and it is the place through which the water flows on to the agriculture fields of the village. The Pedda Tuumu is a wonderful construction, constructed completely using the rocks and it is very strong even today.
        Pedda Tuum is recognized as a unique historical architectural monument by the Namaste Telangana, regional news paper and details about this tuum got published under Sunday Special weekly, 'Sirf Hamara' section.

The Pedda Puumu is in the middle of the lake and it is on the road side of Ramayampet to Gajwel main road. 
You can View the 3-D model of this architecture from Google Earth.
People, students of this village comes to pedda tuumu often to rejuvenate and see the beauty of the lake and surrounding adavi area.
 During the pedda batukamma pandaga, the women of the village come here and leave their Batukammas in this lake from the Pedda Tuumu. The Ganesh Immersion, Durga Devi Immersion also takes place from the same Pedda Tuumu. The people of this village also come here to swim in the lake. Sometimes they jump from the top of the pedda tuum into the water. This lake is also the best place for the villagers to do fish farming.
