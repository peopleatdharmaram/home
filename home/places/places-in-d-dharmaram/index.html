Cheruvu
D.Dharmaram cheruvu is one of the biggest lakes in Ramayampet mandal. This lake is the key for the agriculture in the village. This cheruvu has Pedda Tuum, through which the water flows on to the agriculture fields of the village. 
The key two places in the cheruvu are
    1. Peddatuum
    2. Alugu
